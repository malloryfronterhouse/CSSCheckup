Prolific Serial Killers

Killers YOU have probably never heard of, but should've!

  1. Issei Sagawa

    Issei Sagawa was one seriously sick ad twisted little dude. He was not only a murder but also a cannibal and necrophiliac. His story is intriguing though considering he is a free man today! Queue the collective panic attack. We've attached a video above if you'd like to learn more or you can look here if you prefer to read!


  2. Giulia Tofana

    Coming in hot as the world's most (allegedly) prolific serial killer, regardless of gender, is Giulia Tofana. She is rumored to be responsible for roughly 600 deaths by poisoning of Italian men. It's worth noting that not much is truly known about Miss Tofana, as most records have been lost or destroyed. The video above is a great rundown of the deets we do know and there is more information here as well!


  3. Alexander Pichushkin

    Alexander Pichushkin is definitely one for the books. He is suspected to have killed as many as 60 people. His ultimate goal was to kill 64 because it would match the number of squares on a chess board. This, coupled with his obsession with playing chess, spawned him the nickname 'The Chessboard Killer'. Above is an excellent video about his life, murders, and imprisonment. You can also use this as a jumping off point for your own research into this Russian maniac!
